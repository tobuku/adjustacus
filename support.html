Using the App

Getting Started

Open the app and tap the camera button on the main screen. Point your phone at any room or area — a living room, kitchen, office, or storage space — and tap to capture. There are no special lighting or positioning requirements. For best results, take the photo from a position where most items in the room are visible.
Adjustacus recognizes over 200 item categories including furniture, electronics, appliances, lighting fixtures, window treatments, artwork, rugs, and more. The AI is trained on household and commercial contents typical to insurance and property documentation. Items that are partially obscured or very small may not always be detected.

Reports & Exports

Your Documentation

Adjustacus exports reports as PDF or CSV. The PDF is a formatted, professional document suitable for submission to insurance carriers, legal proceedings, or personal records. The CSV is a structured spreadsheet you can open in Excel, Google Sheets, or any claims management software.
Tap the share button on any completed report and choose Email. You can enter your carrier's claims email address directly. The report attaches automatically. You can also save the file to your device and attach it manually to any email, portal upload, or document submission system.
Reports are saved locally on your device and accessible from the Reports tab in the app. You can also export them to iCloud Drive, Files, or any storage service connected to your iPhone to keep a backup.
Replacement values are based on current market pricing data and are designed to be defensible starting points for insurance documentation. Values reflect typical retail replacement cost, not original purchase price or depreciated value. You can adjust any estimate to reflect a specific item's brand, model, or condition before exporting.

Your Information
